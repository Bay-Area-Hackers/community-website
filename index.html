Qualifications

You need to:
  • Live in Bay Area (because we are planing on in-person short-term development events.)
  • Have programming experience in at least one language: C, C++, C#, Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, Swift, Kotlin, Dart, Scala, Rust, Ruby, Go and/or Python
  • Have the motivation to learn CS
  • Show respect to other members with various languages/programming levels and diverse background
